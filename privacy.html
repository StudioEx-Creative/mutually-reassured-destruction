Privacy

Plain language, because that is the only kind worth writing. Last updated June 2026.

What we collect

Almost nothing. The artwork is a static page. It sets no advertising or tracking cookies and runs no analytics without your consent.

  • Coalition sign-up: if you add your voice, we store the priorities you ranked and the country you selected. An email address is optional — leave it blank to take part anonymously. We will never sell or share it; it is used only to send you Blueprint and Six Degrees updates if you ticked the box.
  • No account, no profile, no third-party trackers.

Your location ("Your area")

The hazard feed is opt-in. Nothing happens until you press "Show what's happening near me" or type a place into the search box.

  • Your coordinates are used only to query public hazard services as a radius or bounding-box, and are never sent to us or to any other party.
  • Those services are: GDACS, USGS, Open-Meteo (weather + air quality), and, where configured, NASA FIRMS. Each receives only the location you are querying about, the same as any weather site.
  • Your coordinates are held in memory for the session only. Nothing is written to a server. Polling pauses when the tab is hidden and stops when you leave.

Where the data lives

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Coalition sign-ups, when the backend is enabled, are stored in a locked-down Supabase table that accepts only the fields above. Until then, sign-ups live only in your own browser's local storage.

Your choices

To remove a coalition sign-up, or for any correction, email hello@studioex.co.
