# Mutually Reassured Destruction — Data Sources & Audit Trail

*An artwork by Blueprint · Produced by StudioEX · 2026.*

Every figure in the piece is sourced to a primary authority and carries an access
date. This file is the master audit trail. The same source ids appear in
`js/data.js` (the `SOURCES` manifest), are rendered inline on each indicator
detail panel, and are summarised in the methodology footer — three layers of
attribution, the same standard as *The Cost of War*.

**All figures verified against primary sources in June 2026.** Nothing is
invented or inflated. Where authoritative sources disagree, the most recent
peer-reviewed value is used and the disagreement noted.

### Heat stripes — the one series to swap before public launch
The warming-stripes block (`HEAT_STRIPES` in `js/data.js`) is generated by
interpolating between **sourced anchor years** (verified anchors include
2024 = 1.60 °C, 2023 = 1.48 °C, 2016 = 1.29 °C, plus decadal points). It is a
faithful visualisation of the Berkeley Earth annual record, but it is **not** the
authoritative per-year CSV. Before public promotion, replace it with the official
[Berkeley Earth annual global mean series](https://berkeleyearth.org/data/) (or
HadCRUT5 / NASA GISTEMP) via `scripts/fetch.mjs`. Concept credit: Ed Hawkins,
[#ShowYourStripes](https://showyourstripes.info/).

## Chapter 03 · The Ledger
- **States** (`LEDGER_STATES`): cumulative (since 1850), annual (2024) and per-capita CO₂ from [Our World in Data / GCP](https://ourworldindata.org/co2-emissions); 1.5 °C ratings from [Climate Action Tracker](https://climateactiontracker.org/countries/). **REFRESH** annually.
- **Carbon Majors** (`CARBON_MAJORS`): 122 entities ≈ 72% of fossil + cement CO₂ since 1854 (canonical figure); 2024 update: ~169 active producers ≈ 80% of 2024 emissions. [InfluenceMap / Carbon Majors](https://carbonmajors.org/).
- **Subsidies**: $7 trillion (2022, explicit + implicit), [IMF](https://www.imf.org/en/Topics/climate-change/energy-subsidies).
- **Policy timeline**: dates are matters of record (UNFCCC, Kyoto, Paris, COP outcomes, US IRA). Lobbying context: [InfluenceMap](https://influencemap.org/), [OpenSecrets](https://www.opensecrets.org/), EU Transparency Register.

## Chapter 04 · The Forecast (`SCENARIOS`, `FORECAST_IMPACTS`)
No projection is freehanded. Every value carries its scenario and a range.
- Current Policies ≈ 2.6 °C (range 2.0–3.1); Pledges ≈ 2.6 °C — [Climate Action Tracker, Nov 2025 update](https://climateactiontracker.org/global/temperatures/) (note: little change for four straight years).
- 1.5 °C Aligned: [IPCC AR6](https://www.ipcc.ch/report/ar6/syr/) SSP1-1.9; IEA NZE — net zero CO₂ ~2050.
- High-emission SSP5-8.5 ≈ 4.4 °C (3.3–5.7) — IPCC AR6, shown **only** as an upper-bound stress test; the IPCC notes it is no longer the most likely trajectory.
- Sea level, ice-free Arctic, coral, heat exposure: IPCC AR6 WG1. Displacement ranges: [World Bank Groundswell](https://www.worldbank.org/) (up to 216 million internal climate migrants by 2050).

## §4 · Live hazard feed — endpoints used
All free, keyless, CORS-friendly, called client-side (`js/hazard.js`):
- [USGS Earthquake](https://earthquake.usgs.gov/fdsnws/event/1/) — GeoJSON.
- [Open-Meteo Forecast](https://open-meteo.com/en/docs) — current temperature.
- [Open-Meteo Air-Quality](https://open-meteo.com/en/docs/air-quality-api) — PM2.5 / AQI.
- [Open-Meteo Archive](https://open-meteo.com/en/docs/historical-weather-api) — local normal → today's anomaly.
- [Open-Meteo Geocoding](https://open-meteo.com/en/docs/geocoding-api) — place search.
- [GDACS](https://www.gdacs.org/) — global disaster alerts (attempted; graceful fallback if CORS-blocked).
- Reverse geocode label: [BigDataCloud](https://www.bigdatacloud.com/) (keyless).

**Need a free key (wire before public launch):** [NASA FIRMS](https://firms.modaps.eosdis.nasa.gov/api/) active fires (MAP_KEY) and [OpenAQ v3](https://docs.openaq.org/) air monitors. Route these through a tiny proxy/Worker so the key isn't exposed, or accept the keyed client call. See README.

## Refresh calendar
- **Monthly:** Mauna Loa CO₂ (NOAA).
- **Each Jan:** prior-year global temperature (Copernicus/WMO/Berkeley); GCP annual budget (released Nov/Dec).
- **Each Feb / Sept:** Antarctic / Arctic sea-ice minima (NSIDC).
- **Annually:** OHC, sea level, ice-sheet & glacier balance, forest loss (GFW, ~April), ledger shares, CAT ratings.
- **On each CAT global update:** forecast warming numbers.
